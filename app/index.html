A Little About Me...

Discovering my passion for programming was a very happy accident. Before becoming a web developer, I did everything from event planning to email marketing to fundraising to database management for Boston-area nonprofits. I eventually realized that my work managing the organization's email marketing was what I enjoyed most and started teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. From there, I decided to enroll in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, which I consider the best decision of my career.

I'm a long-time Boston resident with varied interests: food, running, brewing beer, whitewater rafting, Boston sports, ballet, and art.
