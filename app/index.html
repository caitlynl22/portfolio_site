A Little About Me...

Before becoming a web developer, I was a nonprofit event planner/jack-of-all-trades. Discovering my love of programming was a happy accident when I realized managing my organization's email marketing was what I enjoyed most. I went on to teach myself HTML and CSS and then to the Web Development Immersive program at General Assembly. I'm now excited to hone my skills, learn new technologies, and begin my new career.

I'm a long-time Boston resident with varied interests that include food, running, brewing beer, whitewater rafting, Boston sports, ballet, and art.
